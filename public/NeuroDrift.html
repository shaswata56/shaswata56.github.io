Reading the numbers

Fitness = how far a car drives, measured in laps of the track (distance travelled ÷ track length). Half a lap = 0.50.

Best ever = the highest fitness any single car has reached on this track — and from gen shows which generation produced it. That car is your champion (★ Watch best · Share).

This gen = the best fitness in the current generation only. Laps now = laps completed by the car you're watching.

A car dies if it leaves the track or stops making progress. New track resets these numbers, but your trained drivers carry over. Each generation also adds fresh-random and bold-mutated cars, so you'll always see variety.

Reset evolution?
This permanently erases the driver you’re currently training and starts again from scratch. Drivers saved in your garage are not affected. This can’t be undone.
